Cereal Bars

  • 1 cup old fashioned oats
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp. table salt
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 stick unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 10 tbsp. desired jam for filling, or you can use apple sauce or mashed fresh berries for natural sugar
Instructions
  1. Chop up oats using food processor or Tupperware Power Chef
  2. Mix together chopped oats, flour, salt, and baking powder
  3. In a seperate small bowl stir together egg, melted butter, vanilla, and honey
  4. Mix wet ingredients into dry
  5. Roll out dough until 1/4 inch thick
  6. Using spatula or dough scraper cut rectangles out of rolled out dough, approximatley 3 1/2 inches by 4 inches
  7. Spread jam or other filling into center of each rectangle
  8. Gently fold dough over filling into thirds and gently press down ends to close bars
  9. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  10. Bake for 15 - 18 minutes or until lightly browned.
